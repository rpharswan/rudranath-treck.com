Rudranath is Temple Of Lord Shiva in Uttarakhand Chamoli Region. Lord Shiva face worshiped in Rudranath Temple. It only opens for 6 months a year. It is one of the Panch Kedar and the only Shaktipeeth in Uttarakhand. Situated about 3200 meter above sea level. It is one of the hard and toughest treck of 21 kms. from Saggar village which is about 5 km away from Gopeshwar Town.
